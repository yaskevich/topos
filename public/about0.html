About project

Τόπος – the tool for exploring and visualizing geospatial data about place names located on the territory of Republic of Belarus.

Current status: working prototype (pre-pre-alpha)

Datasets included:

  • List of place names, with coordinates (90%)
  • Current administrative division of the state – boroughs, cities, districts, and regions (100%)
  • List of place names renamings during Soviet age (60%)
  • Historical data about Belarusian cities from ancient times till today
    (this data layer even wasn't expected to be full)
  • Statistics: data of census of 2009 – returns concerning language preferences, population density (related to districts – from Wikipedia)
  • Additional layers to highlight naming patterns and renaming trends
    (topography, railway system, water resources – from OpenStreetMap)

Under the hood: Perl (Mojolicious), JavaScript (D3, JQuery), HTML/CSS (Twitter Bootstrap)
